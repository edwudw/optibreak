OptiBreak Chrome Extension

Take regular breaks for healthier eyes!
View on GitHub

Features

  • Reminds you every customizable interval (default: 20 minutes) to take a 20-20-20 eye break.
  • Shows a real-time countdown to your next break when you click the extension icon.
  • Easy-to-use options page to set your preferred reminder interval.
  • Accessible, high-contrast design for all users.
  • Works entirely offline and respects your privacy.

How it works

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click the OptiBreak icon to see the countdown to your next break.
  3. Click Options in the popup to set your preferred reminder interval.
  4. Every interval, you'll get a notification reminding you to look away for 20 seconds.
